TL;DR

Mean hand L2 drops from 124 mm -> 8.7 mm; hand chamfer 125 -> 13.9 mm; object L2 72 -> 17 mm after four coordinated fixes that landed in commit 16839a2:
  1. Enable GPS Laplacian-eigenvector skip connection at the trajectory head (use_gps=True by default; was False).
  2. Rest-pose canonical hand mesh at inference (matches training; previous run fed v6 posed-frame-0 canonical).
  3. Tight overfit window (frames 30-34 only) via new --frame-start / --max-frames flags.
  4. Sliding S=4 causal window at eval instead of S=1 — aligns with main-branch per-view convention.
Result: hand L2 is now on par with the v6 baseline's 8.5 mm floor and shape is preserved throughout the window.
