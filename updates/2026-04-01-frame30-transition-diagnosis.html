What The Trace Shows

Hand Transition

Hand stays non-empty until step 40. At step 41, the sparse field becomes fully nonnegative, mesh extraction returns zero vertices, and geo_loss drops to zero.

Object Transition

Object already hits empty mesh at step 27 while the field is still mixed_sign. By step 37 it becomes fully nonnegative. This means “no extracted surface” can happen before the entire field loses all negative values.

Objective Gap

Once mesh extraction fails, geo_loss = 0 and only reg_loss remains. That gap first appears at step 41 for hand and step 27 for object.

Coordinate Implication

GT camera projection is still valid and dense. The first broken layer in this experiment is the decoded field / extraction path, not world -> camera or camera -> image.
