Extrinsic Convention Verdict
c2w (camera-to-world). Evidence: extrinsic[:3,3] translations are <0.014 across all 30 frames (vs depth range 0.5–2.0 m). World-z of pts is 100% positive (sign_neg_frac = 0.0 for all frames) under both c2w and w2c interpretations — the scene is a near-frontal robot arm. Camera motion is small relative to depth, so world-z ≈ camera-z directly. No inversion needed for depth rendering.
