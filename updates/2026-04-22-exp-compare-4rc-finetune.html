Depth: both finetunes cut abs_rel by ~3–4× and push δ<1.25 from 0.29 → 0.88 (LoRA) / 0.93 (full). Full FT's extra capacity delivers a modest depth advantage.
Camera: LoRA wins decisively on rotation (37° vs full's 124°). Full FT's unrestricted backbone update disturbs the pretrained cam_dec; LoRA's limited capacity preserves it. Translation-direction error collapses to ~11° for both FTs (from 102° pristine).
Caveat: pristine rotation error of 165° suggests a coord-frame convention mismatch between our GT extrinsic (DexYCB world→cam 4×4) and Arc's pose_enc (9-dim relT+quat+FoV). The relative ordering pristine ≫ full ≈ LoRA still holds, but absolute rotation numbers should be interpreted within the same evaluator.
