Key discoveries

FIX H/W train/eval mismatch was the dominant confound (6× gap)

Morning eval at dataloader-native 518×518 on a 16-frame clip produced hand 57-109 mm / obj 32-110 mm for the 4 LoRA cells. Re-running with --full-sequence --fixed-h 378 --fixed-w 504 (matching 4RC's load_images(size=512, patch_size=14) rectangular crop of DexYCB 480×640) collapsed everything to 12-18 hand / 5-9 obj mm. hoi3r_eval.py now supports these flags natively.

FAIL All LoRA cells regress vs the frozen baseline on training metrics

Frozen baseline pt-9fa652zu reaches best-of-last-20 hand 5.08 / obj 3.57 mm. Every LoRA cell is worse: E1 8.72/5.00, E4 9.66/5.57, E1+scene 8.31/3.93, E1+patchctx 9.17/4.62. The ViT-G wasn't a bottleneck at this regime; LoRA capacity adds memorization noise. Phase-1 decision = NO-IMPROVEMENT.

FINDING Scene-loss is the only LoRA intervention that partially recovers

Within the LoRA family, E1 + --scene-loss-weight 0.1 (single-forward shared feats) beats E1 by 16% on eval hand and 14% on eval obj (12.4/4.8 vs 14.7/5.6). Obj 3.93 on train is the closest any LoRA cell gets to the baseline's 3.57. Aux depth+camera forces LoRA into scene-geometry preservation rather than motion-only shortcut.

FAIL pt-9fa652zu checkpoint + arms A/B checkpoints don't load into current HEAD

Three checkpoints predate the 2026-04-23 motion-head rewrite (commit 58532a2) and the 2026-04-22 handedness two-slot fix (commit 7cdd0f5a). State-dict keys don't match (old self_attn/cross_attn/adaln.proj/mlp vs new cross_block/self_block/attn.toq-tok-tov/LayerScale). Fresh retrain at current HEAD is the only unblock — BASELINE_frozen_separate.sh staged.

FINDING pt-drxe2i2e cl=4-train eval at cl=63 produces 40× regression (6.2 → 432 mm obj)

archmatch-bs8 was trained at a fixed clip length of 4 frames. Evaluated at the full 63-frame sequence it degrades ~40× on obj (best-of-last-20 train 6.21 → full-seq eval 431.63). Confirms memory project_cl_train_eval_mismatch: train cl determines inference cl. The 4 LoRA cells were trained at variable T ∈ [2, 16] so they generalize to 63 frames without this failure.

FIX Plotly v3 renderer now overlays pred + GT on every frame

hoi3r_offline_render.py v3 mode now reads predictions.pt's hand_traj_gt / object_traj_gt and emits gray (rgba 120/160 @ 0.55 alpha) scatter traces underneath the colored pred points on every slider frame. Reveals pred-vs-GT drift per-frame without a separate comparison video. Skips silently if GT keys are absent.
