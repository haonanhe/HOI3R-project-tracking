Conclusions

  • Latent reconstruction loss works: Training the token bridge (4 tensors, ~66K params) for 200 steps recovers recognizable mesh shapes from the full STream3R pipeline
  • Resolution 256 is critical: At res=8, the pipeline had only 1 latent token (meaningless). At res=256, 640+ tokens provide enough information for faithful reconstruction
  • Decoded shapes match GT: Post-training hand shows clear fingers/palm, object shows correct bounding geometry
  • Vertex count gap remains: Post-training produces ~24K verts vs reference 170K. The bridge preserves latent features but not perfectly — some information loss in the 32→1024→32 projection
  • Next steps: Unfreeze STream3R global_blocks for cross-modal fusion; increase training steps; explore direct mesh supervision
